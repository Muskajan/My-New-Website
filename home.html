Vission

Refugees and immigrants, especially those who have experienced war and persecutionn, leading self-sufficient and dignified lives in a socially inclusive society.

Mission

AWO works with refugees and immigrants, particularly those who have experienced wars and persecution, with special focus on women and their families. Its mission is to improve their quality of life, and to promote their social and economic inclusion in order to enable them to become contributing members of society and to live in dignity.

Values

Access and equality, dignity and respect, social inclusion and collaboration.

All our programs and services are free of charge. Please call for an appointment.
